# Statistical charts
The Soccer Theory Suite provides some statical charts. The charts for single games are integrated in SoTheReplay.
## Possession bar chart
For a single game, the possession bar chart shows the ball possession percentage for each team and the final score.

<img width="485" height="501" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c43c4a92-84c6-4f92-81bc-4d088c7ae8de" />

Furthermore, the chart shows the percentage of numerical superiority for each team if the data is available.
## Mean Possession timeline
For a single game, the mean possession timeline shows the mean possession percentage at successive frame periods and the goal moments for each team.

<img width="550" height="560" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4d47e867-23c6-40cf-a70d-47ef873965e6" />

## Continuous Possession timeline
For a single game, the continuous possession timeline shows the intervals with continous ball possession for each team and the goal moments. 

<img width="725" height="528" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5df44c90-f074-4c3d-bb54-83c70982f9ec" />

The exact frame intervals respectively the exact goal moment are popped up passing the mouse cursor over the chart elements.
